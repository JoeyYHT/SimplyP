Parameter files for the scenario runs for INCA-P in the Tarland catchment
Run for the period 1981 - 2010

Produced by Leah Jackson-Blake. Originally carried out for the Defra LM03 Integrated Modelling project.
Reproduced in Jackson-Blake et al. (2016) EM&S.

Scenarios:
M00: baseline (same as calibration parameters)
M2a: 10% P reduction to agricultural land
M2b: 25% reduction
M2c: 50% reduction
M2d: 100% reduction
